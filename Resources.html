Resources

Being at a new school, it is vital to know about all of the resources the school has to offer academically, socially, physically and so forth, so we have compiled them together for you here.

  1. Uncle Joe's: Uncle Joe's provides confidential peer counseling for any Wash U student who has a problem or concern they would like to discuss
  2. Cornerstone: Giving students access to academic tutoring, help preparing for tests, disability resources, and financial aid help.
  3. Student Union (SU): Wash U's student representatives running many facetes of the school. SU's goal is to advocate for students, allocate resources and plan community wide programs.
  4. Fitness
    • Athletic Center (AC): The building on main campus to get a good workout in, play some basketball or watch many of Washington Univerity's sports teams play.
    • S40 Fitness Center (known as The Estrogym): The workout facility on the S40. The Estrogym doesn't have as many workout options as the AC, but it a great options instead of walking all the way to campus for some students.
